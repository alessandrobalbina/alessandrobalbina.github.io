About

I am a Ph.D. student in Economics and Business (41st cycle) at the University of Cagliari.
My academic journey is driven by a passion for data-driven sustainability. In 2025, I earned my Master’s degree in Management and Monitoring of Sustainable Tourism, defending a thesis that explored tourism data and estimated the underground tourism economy in the city of Cagliari. That same year, I merged research with practical impact by contributing to the "Sardinian Roots" project (e.INS), designing tailored tourism experiences for the global Sardinian diaspora.

Currently, my research focuses on the adoption and use of artificial intelligence by tourists for planning and booking travel experiences, with a specific focus on AI chatbots using mixed qualitative and quantitative methodologies.

At Sinergie-SIMA 2026, I received an award as Outstanding Young Scholar Reviewer.
